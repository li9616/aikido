Detailed Description

Wraps an aikido StateSpace into a space recognized by OMPL.

Parameters
_sspaceThe aikido::statespace::StateSpace to expose to OMPL
_interpolatorAn aikido interpolator used by the interpolate method
_dmetricThe distance metric to use to compute distance between two states in the StateSpace
_samplerA state sampler used to sample new states in the StateSpace
_boundsConstraintA Testable used to determine whether states fall with in bounds defined on the space.
_boundsProjectionA Projectable that can be used to project a state back within the valid boundary defined on the space.
